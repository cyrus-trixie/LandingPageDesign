About BlackIvy

IndigoHomes is a BlackIvy endeavor. BlackIvy is a value-driven U.S. company that builds, operates and scales businesses that drive long-term growth and resilient value in essential sectors – housing, food, healthcare, infrastructure and logistics – in Sub-Saharan Africa. Anchored in Ghana, Kenya and Tanzania, BlackIvy is relentless about quality, conscious about costs and driven to see its products and services enjoyed by more and more people. BlackIvy has offices in Accra, Dar es Salaam, Nairobi, New York and Washington D.C.
